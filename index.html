What is Terrorism?

Terrorism is an ideology of violence and a practice of influencing the decision-making of authorities, local governments, or international organizations, associated with intimidation of the population and other forms of illegal violent actions.

Terrorist acts cause mass human casualties, lead to the destruction of material and spiritual values, and sow enmity between states and peoples.

Signs of Terrorist Attack Preparation

  • Appearance of ownerless packages, bags, boxes in public places
  • Detection of suspicious objects with wires, timers, batteries
  • Suspicious behavior of people, inadequate reaction to law enforcement actions
  • Suspicious or abandoned vehicles near public buildings
  • Distribution of suspicious materials or calls for violence

What to Do in Case of Terrorist Threat

  1. Pay attention to suspicious objects or suspicious behavior of people
  2. When detecting suspicious objects, immediately report this to law enforcement authorities
  3. Do not accept packages and bags from unknown persons
  4. Do not leave your belongings unattended
  5. In case of evacuation, follow the instructions of responsible personnel
  6. If taken hostage, do not provoke terrorists and comply with their demands until help arrives
