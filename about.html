About Our Organization

Together against terrorism and extremism

Our Mission

Our organization works in the field of informing the public about terrorist threats and ways to prevent them. We strive to create a safe society free from extremism and terrorism.

Our mission is to inform citizens, increase their vigilance, and assist law enforcement agencies in preventing terrorist threats.

Areas of Activity

  • Information and educational work with the population
  • Conducting educational events in schools and universities
  • Cooperation with law enforcement agencies
  • Publishing informational materials
  • Monitoring the information space
  • Assistance to victims of terrorist acts
